## Equations

Straight line

$$
y = mx + b
$$

$$
Ax + By + C = 0
$$

Point-slope formula

$$
y - y_1 = m(x-x_1)
$$

$$
m = (y_2 - y_1)/(x_2 - x_1)
$$

Angle between lines

$$
\alpha = arctan[(m_2 - m_1)/(1 + m_2 m_1)]
$$

Distance between two points

$$
d = \sqrt{(y_2 - y_1)^2 + (x_2 - x_1)^2}
$$

Quadratic Equation

$$
x = \frac {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 -4ac}} {2a}
$$
